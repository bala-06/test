About MOU Management System

BIP PROJECT ID: 2657INT424

Project Overview

The MOU Management System is a comprehensive platform designed to streamline the management of Memoranda of Understanding (MOUs) between Bannari Amman Institute of Technology and its partner organizations.

Key Features

  • Digital storage of all MOU documents
  • Automatic expiration tracking and alerts
  • Department-wise categorization
  • Advanced search and filtering capabilities
  • Role-based access control
  • Report generation and analytics

Technical Specifications

Frontend

HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap 5

Backend

Django Framework

SQLite3
